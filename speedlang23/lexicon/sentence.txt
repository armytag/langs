melxʷi tsejuse min tsaʔahwi xʷontse neʔa xʷise munaʃtʃa satsa senu tsamnuse kʷaʃkʷu tsolha

jemi kʷumʔesa ʔawihu heja monʔe soʃu hiʃselas heʔa tuxʷum pinxʷaʃ leh je tsulu

ʔehwa petsotʃo nelpel tuʃjal soʔexʷuh tsumwon lemalpu jepukʷu nuhohe poxʷimtʃo xʷilmu sowuʃ wimjehu pe kʷenʃi kʷomʃaʃsim noto lomlutʃo hoswoxʷ

jeʔope nokʷi we netʃaʔu

meʃe jokʷahkʷe juhtaltsol ʔeʃusum

nuʔetsa lejum tʃuʔilti niha kʷomtsu muwasom tsaxʷuxʷe waʃelte

ʃe kʷeʃ kʷatsi memahon ʔesnati wuje sespiʃun xʷintʃuʃ hutʃoʃo sowu ʃehalhe tson si juʃlexʷlo

tansu puʃtsa ʃetsi jiluh hola wopu lohji suhjaʔiʃ

nintsuxʷi xʷajoʃ ʔise setʃaxʷe joʃkʷenlu kʷowa sis tʃiʔoxʷa ju pilamkʷi sepitsaʃ

xʷus tasapih wo tʃihinlu weʔoʃ tʃalinma
