Day 274: September 30, 2024

Goal: Plan a strategy for comparative and superlative adverb forms

Note: You do not need dedicated forms to be able to express a comparison of adverbs.

Tip: You may create multiple templates to demonstrate how your language can express these ideas using different strategies.

Work focus: Organize/Plan/Structure

Consolidate your ideas from the past two days and make a plan for comparative and superlative strategies for adverbs in your conlang. As with adjectives, you may not have dedicated forms or constructions for these situations; rather, you may provide multiple strategies for how you might express comparative meanings using other strategies (e.g. different ways to word clauses to make it clear a comparison is going on).
