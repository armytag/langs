Goal: Entry for Lexember 10th

If you started this month out having decided not to share your work online but now feel motivated to share, go ahead and jump in! It’s okay to do a big “here’s what I’ve done so far” post and then have more regular posts after that. It’s also okay to just jump right in to whatever day it is. I promise you it is more than fine to start Lexember with your Day 10 entry.

As someone who has regularly participated for 6-7 years now and who follows the Lexember tag online, I can tell you I don’t notice when people miss days or jump in and out. What I do notice is the new entries as they come up, and I am always delighted to see what people have been working on.
