Work focus: Create/Make/List

If you are working on glyphs, today is the final day set aside for glyph evolution. That doesn’t mean you will necessarily finish all the glyphs you need—if you’re working on a pure syllabary or logography, you may need a lot more time! That way, on any day that you have extra time, you can dip back into the glyphs and work on one or two here or there to keep expanding your system.
