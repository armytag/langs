Day 129: May 8, 2024

Goal: Create adjective forms in your language

Note: These may be basic, derived, or adjective-like verbs/nouns.

Tip: Create words to describe common features in the conworld.

Work focus: Create/Make/List

Today’s goal is to have some fun creating new adjectives (or adjective-like forms) in your language! Whether you create basic roots, derived/compounded forms, nominal modifiers, or predicative verbs, enjoy expanding your vocabulary. If you’re stuck for ideas of what kinds of words to create, think about modifiers that would come in handy to describe the conworld around your speakers—or that could help describe the speakers themselves.
