Day 355: December 20, 2024

Goal: Entry for Lexember 20th

Work focus: Solidify/Write/Share

Find ways to share your work. Lexember is a great way to share your conlang, and it’s okay if that’s the only way you feel comfortable sharing. If you can share more, though, then all the better! There are many online conlang challenges that run on sites like Reddit or Tumblr, and there are other month-long challenges that tackle areas like grammar.

The more you share, the more you can get feedback and inspire other conlangers.
