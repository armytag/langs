Day 337: December 2, 2024

Goal: Entry for Lexember 2nd

Work focus: Solidify/Write/Share

Quite often, I find I have the most energy and attention for Lexember at the beginning of the month. Though 31 words doesn’t feel like much when you begin, it can start to feel like a lot as you get into the middle of it—especially if you celebrate any holidays in December!

If that spike of energy at the beginning of a project sounds familiar, I encourage you to use the extra energy to work ahead. You can make a few extra entries or start laying plans for a new derivation or whatever—whatever might help you push toward the finish line later in the month.
