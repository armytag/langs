Day 362: December 27, 2024

Goal: Entry for Lexember 27th

Work focus: Solidify/Write/Share

Another way to continue using your language after Conlang Year ends is to translate texts you enjoy, whether they are excerpts from books/articles you enjoy, monologues from movies/shows, random sentences you find delightful, or bits of conversation.

If you enjoy writing in your language but don’t want to translate from another language, you can also consider journaling in your conlang. It would be a surefire way to get more comfortable using your language and can help make you more fluent in it, if that’s a goal you aspire to.
