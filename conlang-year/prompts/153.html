Day 153: June 1, 2024

Goal: Make a plan for adverbs in your conlang

Note: Include ideas for basic roots but also for more complex forms.

Tip: You may be strategizing adverb-like elements.

Work focus: Organize/Plan/Structure

Organize your brainstorming notes from the past few days and make a plan for adverbs in your conlang. Make sure you think about what kinds of words you want to have as basic roots, but also make a plan for more complex ones. For instance, you might map out how compounds that function as adverbs are typically formed.

If you decided not to create adverbs in your language (or to only create a few of them), then your planning will focus on other constituents that can convey adverb-like meaning. Plan out how you might use adposition phrases, noun phrases, or other constituents as adverbial elements.
