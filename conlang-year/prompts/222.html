Goal: Expand sections on nominal modification

Work focus: Solidify/Write/Share

Today’s goal focuses on documentation, expanding the section (or sections) on nominal modification to incorporate details about nonfinite structures that modify head nouns.

Depending on how your structures work, this may be an expansion of information in your relative clause section. You might just need to point out that your language requires relative clauses and does not have adposition and/or nonfinite modifiers and then give a few examples. Or you might need to describe reduced relative clause structures that allow phrasal forms to modify nouns.

If these modifiers are distinct from relative clauses and other structures you’ve described, then it might be best to create a new section for them in your grammar.
