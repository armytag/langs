Day 336: December 1, 2024

Goal: Entry for Lexember 1st

Work focus: Solidify/Write/Share

From here on out, the rest of the Conlang Year challenge is the same daily task: create and share a word in your conlang for Lexember. Just don’t forget to update your dictionary as you go!

You can share your Lexember entries via your favorite social media sites (tag them if you do so others can find them), but you could also share them in a smaller community of friends. Whatever you choose, I hope you find a place to share your work because Lexember is all about celebrating each other and our conlangs.

At this point, you’ve created a full, working language, and you’ve got a language document describing its features. So as you share your Lexember work, I also encourage you to consider sharing your grammar document and/or dictionary—perhaps by submitting it to Fiat Lingua!
