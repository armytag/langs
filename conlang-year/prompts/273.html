Goal: Explore options for superlative adverb forms

Note: Now consider adverbs in superlative situations, like “most joyfully.”

Tip: Manner adverbs are most likely to participate in comparative/superlative constructions.

Work focus: Learn/Brainstorm/Try

Today’s goal builds on yesterday’s explorations by adding superlative adverb forms. Consider how your language might express meanings like “The bear jumped farthest” or “The duck swam most quickly.”

Manner adverbs are most likely to participate in comparative/superlative constructions, but that doesn’t mean other adverbs can’t (e.g. “farther, farthest”).

Today is all about exploration and brainstorming!
