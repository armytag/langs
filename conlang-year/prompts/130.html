Goal: List examples of noun & adjective pairs

Note: These pairs will demonstrate how attributive modifiers are placed in your language.

Tip: These adjectives may inflect to agree with their head noun.

Work focus: Create/Make/List

Today’s goal is to create a list of example noun phrases demonstrating attributive adjective placement relative to the head noun being modified (or attributive modifier placement in general, if you don’t have a category of adjectives in your language). If your adjectives/modifiers will inflect to agree with their head nouns in any way, make sure you remember to apply the inflections in your examples. If your language allows variable orders, be sure to include examples that reflect that feature.
